You can test your knowledge of the Serbian language through the exercises on this page. Choose the exercise you want to do and solve the tasks in order to know at what level your knowledge of Serbian is.

about about about

Before choosing an exercise, you will be able to see information about it. This information includes the number of questions, the type of questions, the difficulty of the exercises... All exercises are automatically available, which means that you can immediately start doing the most difficult exercises. Each task consists of several questions that can be of different types (multiple answers, writing answers, making sentences...). After the solved exercise, you will receive information about your score.

Exercises can be especially helpful, because if you make a mistake, you will be able to see the correct answer to the question and thus learn from your mistakes.
